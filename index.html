Rules & Guidelines
✅Each participant can submit a maximum of 2 ideas
✅Teams must consist of members from the same parent team — cross-team grouping not allowed
✅Each team must have a minimum of 2 and maximum of 5 members
⚠️AI Champions cannot participate as team members — they may only guide teams
✅Ideas must be presented as a clear solution pitch
✅Ideas must be original — any external or pre-built content must be clearly disclosed
❌Use of confidential or sensitive company data is strictly prohibited
✅Use of Accenture-approved AI tools is allowed · Teams may consult AI Champions for guidance
🏆Evaluation criteria: Innovation · Relevance · Feasibility · Business Impact · Clarity
🎤Top 10 shortlisted ideas present to a judging panel — 4–5 minutes + Q&A · Strict time adherence required
ℹ️All submitted ideas may be considered for future development within the organization, with due credit
❌Judges' decisions are final · Any misconduct or plagiarism will lead to disqualification
